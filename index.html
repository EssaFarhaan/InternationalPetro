Oil & Gas Exploration Studies

We Never Give Up

The extensive research and discovery of potential drilling and extraction sites.

We focus on the upstream segment as the most capital-intensive part of oil and gas and the segment of enormous uncertainties to tackle.

International Petro has readily available specialized task teams of global experts maintaining strategic alliances with several international leading firms. This diversity allows us to flexibly handle every aspect of the petroleum industry as it develops.

Our mission is to utilize technology and our worldwide experience to optimize oil and gas exploration and production. We offer our clients high quality services and solutions in specific areas of oil and gas production optimization. We provide value added products and services in the fields of geophysics, geology, formation evaluation, and petroleum engineering to provide our clients a better understanding of their reservoir systems, to reduce uncertainty and risk and hence maximize hydrocarbon recovery. Our services include

  • Integrated Reservoir Studies.
  • Structural Modeling and Stratigraphic Modeling.
  • Petrophysical Modeling.
  • Reservoir Engineering and Dynamic Modeling.
  • Well Modeling and Diagnostics.
  • Full field Reservoir Simulation.

We analyze how artificial intelligence changes a significant part of the energy sector, the oil and gas industry. On review of existing applications, we outline the most recent trends in developing AI-based tools and identify their effects on accelerating and de-risking processes in the industry.
